🚫 NEVER PAY VENDORS DIRECTLY — ALWAYS PAY FROM/THROUGH TRUST WALLET 🚫

If you buy from Cashapp, Venmo, Zelle, Coinbase, etc. and send to your vendor directly, you risk getting your account banned. Always send from your personal wallet like Trust Wallet.

💳 Payment Options & Holds

📱 Install Trust Wallet on your phone if you haven't already.

🤔 Which Crypto To Use? How To Buy Crypto?

🪙 Two Kinds Of Crypto:

  • Non-Stablecoin: ETH, SOL, TRON, BTC ($1 = fluctuates)
  • Stablecoin: USDC or USDT ($1 = $1, needs gas to send)
💡 ANSWER: Whichever your vendor takes with the lowest fees — ask them!

💸 Buy & Send Crypto

🧪 Always start with a test transaction ($20–30)

🛣️ Stablecoin Route — Buy 2 Things, Predictable:

  1. Buy USDT/USDC + gas (SOL, ETH, or TRON) from an exchange.
  2. Send both to Trust Wallet.
  3. Send the stablecoin to your vendor from Trust Wallet.

🕐 Holds & Fees For Buying Crypto

🏦 Coinbase / Kraken:

  • Hold Time: Can be instant or up to a week for new users.
  • Fee: 0% for USDC with a bank account on Coinbase.

Most of us use Coinbase to buy USDC, send to Trust Wallet, and just swap it for whatever other kind of stablecoin we need to send to the vendor at the time.

📱 MoonPay, XO Pay, Ramp:

  • Hold Time: Often just a few hours.
  • Fee: ~4% higher, but accepts Apple Pay.

⚙️ How It Works

  1. Have the stablecoin (USDT/USDC) in your Trust Wallet — this is your payment amount.
  2. Have the native currency (SOL/ETH/etc.) in your wallet — this is your gas money.
  3. When you send, Trust Wallet automatically deducts the gas from your wallet.

🧪 Final Tip

Don't be scared! Before sending crypto, always do a small $5 test transaction and have your vendor confirm they received it.
